Introduction

Clean Cuts Barbershop

A modern barbershop with simple interior design, dedicated to giving the cleanest haircuts.

Target audience

Young adults around 18-30 years old looking for a place to get a short style haircut.

They want a trendy, clean haircut at a low price. The store should look minimalistic with some high-contrast art on the walls. They also display high-quality hair products for sale. They should visit the website, pick a haircut, and make a reservation.
