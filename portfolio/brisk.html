Problem Statement

Through a survey at the hackathon, we found that the majority of the attending college students do not micromanage their spendings. They feel that it takes up too much time energy out of their day. Not managing money can lead to overspending, making people another step away from being a successful adult. A good financial management should be visually appealing and functionally effective.

Vision

To create a financial management app usable in any occasion for quick entry of data.
