Color and Typography Design System

Roopairs Product Design // Fall 2020

Color palette and typeface of the redefined design system.

Roopairs is a startup based in San Luis Obispo, innovating a minimal yet powerful service field management software for repair and maintenance companies. As a Product Design Intern, I had the opportunity to redefine their typeface and color palette.

Through interviews, we found that our users experienced readability issues with our app. Myself and Tyler, my co-intern, investigated this problem and found that the interface's contrast levels were insufficient. The app needs a clear UI for users to have a smooth experience.

In my previous quarter as their intern, I worked on reorganizing and cleaning up their prototype files on Adobe XD. Throughout this process, I found inconsistencies in their typography. Sizing and weights were slightly different, and it had a somewhat unclear sense of hierarchy.
